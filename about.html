// Our story

Made for
builders &
dreamers.

DevVault was born in the classrooms of SIES Graduate School of Technology. We're a group of four second-year students who wanted to create a dedicated space for student projects — a place where your work gets seen, appreciated, and used as a stepping stone by those who come after you.

Whether it's a todo app or a full-stack chat application — every project you build tells a story. DevVault makes sure that story reaches the right people.
